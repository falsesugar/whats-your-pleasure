Sexuality and technology are two realms in which women have been told were not made for them; that we don't belong in and cannot own. What's Your Pleasure? is a set of tools and instructions that encourages women to explore and curate their own pleasure system through the building of a unique technological sensual object.

Our physical toolkit comes with a variety of stimulating outputs and inputs for the user to easily create a tactile gadget that is distinctively theirs. This website offers further information and a community for women to learn and share experiences relating to sex and pleasure. Through the reinforcement of technological capabilities, our goal is to provide to women a sense of empowerment and ownership of their individual sexuality and pleasure.
